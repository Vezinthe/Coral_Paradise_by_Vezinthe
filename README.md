This zone was made within 5 days. With this rate, and my experience as a game dev, I can complete this within a week.

This is a coral themed zone where the zone is above the ocean filled with overfilled corals with a variety of colors.
The surface is peaceful and beautiful to encourage builders like me. While the deep region is filled with danger and loot, for players who love adventure.

----------- COMPLETED -----------
Biome 1 - Coral Garden | Lake filled biome inspired by a real life wonder called Lancois Marehenses.
Biome 2 - Winding Reefs | A rocky reef with deep ponds that lead to underground caves. It is a biome that have lots of small openings and isolated ponds, perfect for players who want a "hidden" base feel.

----------- PARTIALLY COMPLETED -----------
Surface Custom Prefabs - 80%
  These refer to the corals that I have created. I plan to do more, especially prefabs with glowing crystals so the zone looks magical and colorful whether it is day or night.

Biome 3 - Catalyst Caves | Large, underground caves filled with glowing crystals and has its own lakes. This region has its own ecosystem and will be filled with hostile mobs, custom prefabs, and custom blocks.
